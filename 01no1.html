About the game

Some of the game features.

Awesome World

Welcome to our World, where there are no boundaries! Kids can play and have fun. For many of our little friends this world will be their first experience being a part of community. It will be a place where kids can socialize and make new friends.

Educational

Learn new things - our little friends can play multiple games where they can learn many many things – from what the constellations look like to some Chinese words - and all this while they have fun and look for new friends.

Modifiable Characters

Modify your character to look the way you like. Change the outfit, color it, get a new hair cut and many many more ways to be different everyday! Make yourself a cute little character! Dress-up nicely and decorate your place with many customizations.

Mini-games

While exploring the world you can go and check our mini-games. You can play on your own or you can challenge other players. Enjoy adventurous world with epic story quests, fun mini-games, crazy puzzles and competitions.

Multiplayer

You will be able to play with people all over the world or with your friends. Challenge them, play the games, make new friends and win! Be part of great tournaments.

Child-proof

Here in Waphoo we are very heartfelt to create a place where families to enjoy and have fun in safe environments. Here kids will have their own little world where they can chat safely and have fun without any worries.

Game Developer

We are looking for motivated and talented game programmers at various experience levels - from junior to lead positions. Your focus will be in programming games from the beginning of development through the entire production cycle. You will work in tight co-operation with the game designers, product managers and the rest of the team. You are responsible of implementing and polishing the features that are needed for the game. In the senior/lead game programmer roles you also have the chance to be responsible for project management for the sub-projects in your area of competency.

  • Game Programming experience on published projects
  • Proven strong C# programming skills / Unity / Android SDK / iOS or other
  • Scripting skills and spare time game projects are considered as plus
  • Passion for making games

  • Ability to work under high pressure deadlines
